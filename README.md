# 🎧 Chinook Digital Music Store: Strategic SQL Analysis

## Business Executive Summary
This project transforms raw transactional data from a digital music store (Chinook Database) into actionable business intelligence. As a **Business & Data Analyst**, I answered three critical strategic questions to optimize operations and revenue.

---

## Deep Dive Analysis

### Question 1: Is our revenue concentrated in a few VIP customers?
**Answer:** Yes. A significant portion of our revenue is driven by a loyal group of top spenders. For instance, our top 5 customers spend an average of **$45+** each, which is substantially higher than the global average. 

**Recommendation:** Implement a VIP Loyalty Program focusing on the top 20% of customers (Pareto Principle) to ensure retention and increase their lifetime value.

### Question 2: Which genres are actually the most profitable?
**Answer:** While **Rock** and **Latin** lead in sales *volume*, they do not have the highest average ticket size. Specific genres like **TV Shows** and **Sci Fi & Fantasy** generate much larger individual transactions.

**Recommendation:** Although niche, we should expand the catalog and promotional efforts for higher-ticket genres (like TV Shows) to increase the overall average order value.

### Question 3: How is our sales support team performing?
**Answer:** The performance among our three Sales Support Representatives is exceptionally competitive. However, **Jane Peacock** is currently the top-performing employee, generating the highest total revenue.
